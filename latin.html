🌾 Latin

Language of the Forum and the Flame — still echoing in our blood, in law, in liturgy, in love.

flamma in corde, verba sub sole,
mens mea carminibus versatur.
“Flame in my heart, words beneath the sun —
my mind is shaped by song.”

This verse is a living tribute to Latin's rhythmic elegance. It blends internal fire with classical balance — the Roman soul in motion.
