📜 Phoenician

Born on scrolls of cedar, carved into coin and sail — the language of the waves and stars.

’l mlk ʿlt — "To El, king above"
“To El, flame above the sails —
may the word carry farther than wind.”

This poetic fragment is inspired by votive inscriptions from Phoenician tablets and ship dedications — a blend of the divine and the drifting.
