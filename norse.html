🪓 Old Norse

The language of gods and warriors, of Odin’s wisdom and Thor’s hammer, of the sagas of old.

Óðinn, allsherjargoð
hǫrðr vegr, valkyrjur fljúga
“Odin, all-father god,
hard is the path, the valkyries fly.”

This fragment channels the spirit of the Viking Age, where gods walked the Earth and the wind carried the songs of battles fought.
