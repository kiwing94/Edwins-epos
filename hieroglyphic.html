🦅 Hieroglyphic Egyptian

Each symbol was a god, a gesture, a glimmer of light between death and rebirth.

𓂀 𓈖𓏏𓊹 𓇋𓏏𓊪𓂋
"ntj pt r’ ḏsr" (He who belongs to the sacred sun-sky)
“I walk as a whisper on the sky’s breath —
carved in silence, born in light.”

This reconstructed phrase pays homage to hieroglyphs as not just language, but **ritual architecture** — sounds sealed in symbols, reborn every time they are seen.
