🧱 Sumerian

The ancient language of Mesopotamia, where the first kings and gods spoke their will into the clay.

𒀭𒂗𒆤 𒌷𒀀𒉿𒀭𒈾𒊒𒆕
“inanna su-ud bi-bu-ud”
“Enki’s breath flows from the heavens,
Inanna’s strength rises from the earth.”

This fragment is drawn from Sumerian hymn-like poetry, invoking the gods and their influence upon both heaven and earth.
