🗿 Oskian

The voice of the Samnite stone — carved into bronze, carried by breath.

aúz aamuíí sakarate
mais eituí ínter meddis
“I call to the sacred flame,
and walk with silence between the worlds.”

Inspired by Oscan votive inscriptions and dedications to unknown gods, this fragment revives the elemental soul of Oskian language — earth, fire, and solemn oath.
