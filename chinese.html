The language of the sages, an art of conciseness, where each word holds its weight in poetry.

靜以修身，儉以養德
－ 孟子
“Cultivate virtue through quietude,
nourish morality through frugality.”
— Mencius

This verse is from Mencius, one of the greatest philosophers in China, teaching the value of introspection and simplicity.
